Shrimp Tacoss

Description

Vamonos!!

A very good tacos with pineapple

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 6 (6 inch) flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Thread shrimp onto skewers; brush with melted butter, and coat with seasoning mix. Grill shrimp until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, about 3 minutes per side.
  2. Place tortillas on the hot grill and heat until lightly grilled, 20 to 30 seconds per side.
